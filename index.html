How to explore

Fly toward any surface. Your speed scales with your distance to it — you will never quite arrive. As you close in, the world rebases: new detail grows beneath you while the structures you left behind become colossal vistas overhead. The terrain style, palette and lights change as you go deeper. There is no lower bound: depth level and total zoom are shown top-left.

Scroll adjusts your speed multiplier. F lets the autopilot dive for you. N generates a fresh universe (shareable via the URL). 1/2/3 set render quality (shadows & light shadows). G toggles light glow. P pauses.

Press H to close.
